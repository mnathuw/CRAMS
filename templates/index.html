CRAMS

The Car Rental Agency Management System (CRAMS) is a computer program to control rental operations in an agency including vehicles, clients, bookings and payments. The objective of using this system is to enhance the quality of service provided to clients, minimize human error, and maximize the use of the fleet and the profits made by the dealership through the use of automation and records ease.
